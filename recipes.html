Preparation Steps

  1. Mound the flour on a clean work surface and scoop out a deep well in the center.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well, add the olive oil and salt, and whisk gently with a fork.
  3. Gradually incorporate the flour from the inner walls of the well until a sticky dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough firmly for 8 to 10 minutes until it becomes completely smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap the dough tightly in plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll the dough out thin using a rolling pin or pasta machine, cut into your desired shapes, and boil in salted water for 2 to 3 minutes.
