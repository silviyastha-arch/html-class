Classic Homemade Pasta


Making fresh pasta from scratch is a deeply rewarding culinary art that requires only a few basic pantry staples. This classic Italian recipe combines simple flour and eggs to create a velvety, tender dough that far surpasses any store-bought variety. Whether rolled thin for delicate ravioli or cut into thick ribbons of fettuccine, this authentic homemade pasta pairs beautifully with your favorite rich sauces.
